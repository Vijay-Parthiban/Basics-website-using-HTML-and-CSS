AS A STARTUP

Scorpion venom has potential medical applications, but its use as a treatment is still in the early stages of research and development. Some potential uses for scorpion venom include: Pain management: Some studies suggest that certain components of scorpion venom can be used to manage chronic pain.

Anti-inflammatory effects: Scorpion venom has been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties that could be useful in treating conditions like arthritis.

Cancer treatment: Researchers are investigating the potential of scorpion venom as a treatment for certain types of cancer, as some components of the venom have been shown to have anti-tumor effects. Heart disease: Scorpion venom has been found to have a beneficial effect on heart function and may have potential as a treatment for heart disease.
